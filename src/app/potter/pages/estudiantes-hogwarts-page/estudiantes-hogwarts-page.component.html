HARRY POTTER TO REPLACE SEVERUS SNAPE AS HOGWARTS PROFESSOR!

In a surprising turn of events, sources close to Hogwarts staff have confirmed that none other than Harry Potter, the Boy Who Lived, is set to take on the role of Defense Against the Dark Arts professor. Following the resignation of the controversial Severus Snape, who held the position with unorthodox methods, it seems that Hogwarts has chosen young Potter as the ideal candidate to prepare students against rising threats.

“He’s always had a knack for dealing with dark creatures and wizards,” commented a Gryffindor student, “so it’s exciting to see him in this new role!” However, some wonder if Potter, at only 18, is truly ready for the challenge of teaching students to defend themselves against dark magic. Will Potter live up to Professor Snape’s legacy, or will he face even greater challenges within the walls of Hogwarts? The Daily Prophet will keep you informed of every spellbinding detail.
